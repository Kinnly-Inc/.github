## What is Kinnly?

Kinnly is one platform for running a K-12 school and keeping its whole community connected — administration, teaching, communication, finance, and AI insight in a single source of truth, replacing the spreadsheets and disconnected apps schools usually stitch together.

Built for global schools, trilingual (English, Arabic, French) with right-to-left layout from day one, and available on web, iOS, Android, and tablet — the same experience for admins, teachers, parents, and students wherever they are.

## What it does

- **Lead** — Run the whole school from one dashboard: attendance, finance, staff, wellness, and every conversation in one place.
- **Connect** — Bring parents, teachers, and students into one shared conversation where nothing gets lost. Every family on one platform.
- **Empower** — Take busywork off teachers with drafting tools, auto-grading, and smart routing, so more time goes to teaching.
- **Excel** — Turn real-time data into decisions, with attendance, grade, and behavior trends surfaced before they become problems.

## Built-in AI

Every AI feature runs on the same foundation — private to your school, trained on your data, and role-aware:

- **AI Teaching Assistant** — drafts exams, homework, and lesson plans. The teacher decides; the AI handles the drafting.
- **AI Chat** — a role-aware assistant trained on your school's policies and content, with a distinct view for every role.
- **Smart Operations** — auto-grading, notification routing, and geo-attendance.
- **Predictive Insights** — attendance patterns, grade trends, and behavior signals flagged early.

AI is an assistant, not a decision-maker. Your school's data stays in your school, and children's data is never used to train models without explicit consent.

## Built for trust

- Encrypted data, daily backups, and storage in the right region for your country's laws.
- Local currencies, local payment methods, and correct tax handling for every region we serve.
- One experience across web, iOS, Android, and tablet.
